Welcome to WeSuggest

A place for students to raise issues and make suggestions about campus, completely anonymous or not. It's entirely up to you.

Please Read Before Posting

Rules and Guidelines

  • No foul language of any kind
  • No harassment or making deragatory statements towards anyone
  • Make a suggestion about anything on campus, for example hire more art professors or build more residence halls
  • Take out some frustration and vent anonymously about your day
  • Once you've made your suggestion, head on over to our Vote on Suggestions page and begin voting on other issues people have raised
  • Or, you can check out the top suggestions on the Top Suggestions page
